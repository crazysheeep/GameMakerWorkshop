Tips

Here are a bunch of tips and common mistakes.

Sprites

1. Zoom in before you start drawing
(view => zoom in/out, or click on the magnifying glasses)
2. You can have up to two colours on hand at once. You can select them by left or right clicking on the colour palette
3. Use the line drawing tool to draw lines (L)
4. Use the shape drawing tool to draw shapes (R for rectangle, I for circle)
5. To make a good circle, start from the top left corner of the sprite, and drag to the bottom right corner
6. If you want to draw a special shape, like a star, use the Polygon tool (P)
7. Use the paint bucket tool to fill in an area with colour (F)

Can't move left or right unless player is jumping?

Gravity is making your player constantly colliding with the floor. Double check how gravity is set up. Gravity should be 1 when the space below the player is empty, and 0 when there is a tile below the player.

My player's foot is stuck in the floor, can't move

This is because you have enabled precise collision checking on your player, or the tile, or both.
Precise collision checking means that GameMaker will use the pixels of the sprite to check for collisions, rather than the entire 32x32 area of the sprite.
Fix 1 - disable precise collision checking.
Fix 2 - redraw your sprite to have less pixels that stick out. Make sure that the character's legs are of equal length, and things like that.

I want to test the second room, but I have to play through the first room before I can get there

Go to your first room, and put an obj_flag right next to the player. Now you can just skip the room! Don't forget to remove the obj_flag when you're done testing!
